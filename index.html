About Me.

Hi, I'm Nathan Leafe

I am very passionate about game development and love challenging myself with exciting and ambitious projects. Having graduated my Batchelor of Design (Games) degree at RMIT university, i am looking forward to the next oppurtunity!

I hope you enjoy my portfolio, I am excited to feature many of my future projects here! I always trying to improve upon prevoius work to ensure my portfolio is a good reflection of my current experience. This perspective is also pertinent to my life outside of Design, where i enjoy reading, games with friends and playing guitar!

LostInTrainstation

A Puzzle Game About Linguistics

LostInTranstation is a 3D puzzle game about linguistics made in Unity. The game was developed in a team of 5 over the course of 6 months where i acted as the teams producer and 3D artist.

SwordGuy

A 2D Action Platformer

SwordGuy is a 2D action-adventure platformer currently still in development with plans for a Dec-2022 release. I am very excited to show more of this WIP project and post bi-montly on the accounts instagram page @Swordguy_game with development updates.

Alonely

A Branching Narrative Thriller

This was my first attempt at a web game using Twine. I am excited to use the software again to create more compelling stories similar to Alonely, with hopes of fully revamping the visuals some time in the future.

Pooks Aquarium

A Small 2D Animation

This project was a very captivating experience spending so much time in Adobe Illustrator and AfterEffects to create a short animation that captures the atmosphere of a populated reef. This project not only taught me alot about the softwares involved, but also gave me the oppurtunity to fill the screen with life in an interesting way.

CoinMunchers

A Fast Paced Co-op Game

CoinMunchers was made as a C# programming challenge for myself to create a multiplayer game that was able to be picked up and put down at events like PAX Australia. I hope to interrate on the game further before i can hopefully showcase it at a gaming event.

Island Refuge

A Stranded Island Adventure

Island Refuge was one of my first games but i am still fond of showcasing it as an comparative work. The game is a short and sweet narrative walking simulator taking place on a deserted island.
